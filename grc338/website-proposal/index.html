Final project proposal

Introduction

Annie's Treats

This website will be for a small local bakery that mostly sells cupcakes named "Annie's Treats." The website will have a homepage themed with colors such as black, white, light pink and light blue. There will be pages displaying examples of the cupcakes, the menu, an about page for the store and staff members, as well as a page for booking special orders.

Target audience

The people who will be using this site will be those who have never been to "Annie's Treats" in person and want to know what they have to offer. As well as give people an option to order their desserts online and pick it up in-store.

The audience's primary task and goals are to find out who the people behind "Annie's Treats" are, what kind of cupcakes and desserts they have to offer, and whether the customer wants to spend their money on the desserts at this bakery.
